Friends is an American television sitcom, created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman. It originally aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004, lasting ten seasons. It was produced by Bright/Kauffman/Crane Productions, in association with Warner Bros. Television.

The ensemble cast stars Jennifer Aniston, Courteney Cox, Lisa Kudrow, Matt LeBlanc, Matthew Perry and David Schwimmer. The show revolves around six friends living in Manhattan.
